Gaining Real-World Experience

Organisation Name : Digi Serve

Abstract

During my one-month internship at Digi Serve, I worked on two key projects. The first involved designing a graphical interface for their team member management system, where I applied and improved my UI/UX design skills. The second project was focused on redesigning a page for an existing software developed by Digi Serve, allowing me to further hone my design abilities while understanding how to integrate new elements into an established system.

Through this experience, I enhanced my problem-solving skills, learned how to incorporate feedback effectively, and developed greater proficiency in tools like Figma. The internship also increased my confidence in tackling real-world design challenges and gave me a deeper understanding of how design can contribute to product development.

Nature of My Role & Responsibilities

  • Designed a graphical interface for Digi Serve's team member management system.
  • Redesigned a page for an existing software developed by Digi Serve.
  • Provided feedback on design iterations and refined solutions based on client input.
  • Collaborated with the project manager and other team members to align design with project goals.
  • Delivered a sample code of the design during the final week to assist with further development.

Key Learnings & Outcome

  • Gained experience in creating user-friendly and intuitive design solutions for real-world use cases.
  • Learned the importance of feedback in refining and improving designs.
  • Enhanced the ability to manage real-world client expectations and project timelines.
  • Successfully integrated design elements into an existing software system.

Skills/Knowledge I Gained

  • Improved proficiency in Figma and UI/UX design principles.
  • Strengthened problem-solving and creative thinking skills.
  • Gained confidence in designing user interfaces that meet both functional and aesthetic requirements.
  • Developed an understanding of design implementation through coding by providing sample code.
  • Learned how to work with and contribute to existing project workflows.

Changes in the Attitudes I Experienced

  • Increased confidence in handling real-world design challenges.
  • Shifted towards a more user-centered design mindset, focusing on practical, functional solutions.
  • Inspired by the community work of teaching disabled individuals IT skills.
  • Developed a proactive attitude towards problem-solving and continuous learning through trial and error.

Recommendations

Although the team has a versatile approach, having someone specialized in UI/UX design would make design projects easier and more efficient. Digi Serve receives only a few projects and maintains some of their previous ones, so they do not require a full-time UI/UX designer. However, to improve their product development process, they could benefit from a dedicated UI/UX designer to handle the design and logic aspects of their projects.

Application

The skills and experience I gained from this internship will be valuable as I continue to develop my career. Working on a real project with real users has given me the confidence to handle complex design challenges. I plan to apply the problem-solving skills I developed in future design projects, and I now feel more equipped to understand and respond to client feedback.

This internship has also reinforced my interest in user-centred design. I learned that good design goes beyond aesthetics; it involves making products functional, accessible, and meaningful for users. This understanding will guide me as I work on future projects and continue to refine my design approach.
